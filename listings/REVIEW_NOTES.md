Soak: Hot Tub Assistant is a free utility (with one optional in-app purchase) that converts a hot tub owner's standard water test readings (free chlorine or bromine, pH, total alkalinity, calcium hardness) into exact corrective dose recommendations scaled to their tub volume. The audience is home hot tub / spa owners maintaining either chlorine or bromine water chemistry. It covers test-and-adjust dosing, after-use sanitizer top-ups, and the weekly shock treatment, with plain-English "re-test" and "don't add more" advisories that catch mis-read test strips. The app makes no medical or therapeutic claims.

No account, login, signup, or network connection is required. Soak is free to download and runs a 48-hour full-feature trial from first launch — every feature is unlocked during the trial, so all functionality is testable immediately on a fresh install with no purchase. After 48 hours the calculators (Test & Adjust, After-Use, Shock) are gated behind a single non-consumable in-app purchase, "Soak Pro (lifetime)" ($3.99), which is restorable via "Restore Purchases" on the paywall. Onboarding is 3 steps (tub volume → sanitizer type → preferred products), then the main screen exposes Test & Adjust, After-Use Dose, and Shock. To exercise the dose engine: open Test & Adjust and enter sample readings such as free chlorine 0.5 ppm and pH 7.8 — the dose recommendations render immediately. All persistence is local UserDefaults; deleting the app wipes it.
